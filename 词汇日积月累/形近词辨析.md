## literal & literary
### literal
[adj]
1. 字面的 literal meaning/sense
2. 逐字的 literal translation 直译
3. 如实的；缺乏想象力的 literal-minded
4. *colloquial* (absolute) her marriage was five years of literal hell 她五年的婚姻生活着实让她吃尽苦头
